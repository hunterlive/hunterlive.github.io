Computer Training Classes

Computer training can help individuals qualify for and obtain jobs that lead to economic self-sufficiency.

Computer training can help individuals qualify for and obtain jobs that lead to economic self-sufficiency. A series of computer technology related training courses are offered that range from six (6) to twelve (12) weeks; 18 class-hours to 36 class-hours respectively. There are three (3) levels of training: basic, intermediate, and advanced. The courses include the following:

  • Introduction to Computers (basic usage and keyboarding)

  • Introduction to the Internet and Electronic Mail (Email)

  • Introduction to Word Processing (Microsoft Word)

  • Introduction to Spreadsheets (Microsoft Excel)

  • Introduction to Multimedia Presentations (Microsoft PowerPoint)

  • Introduction to Database Management (Microsoft Access)
